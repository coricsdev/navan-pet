how to use the protocol

a simple, repeatable daily practice. consistency is the mechanism — not a single chew.

01

choose your chew

rotate between the three chews in the core bundle — rabbit ears, rabbit stick, and duck neck — based on your dog's size and chewing session of the day. each provides a slightly different texture and duration.

02

give daily or near-daily

consistency is everything. one chew per day — or most days — is the standard ritual. the mechanical action compounds over time. always supervise while chewing.

03

watch it compound

most pet parents notice fresher breath within the first 25 days. cleaner-looking teeth by day 50. sustained oral support ongoing. this is long-term dental investment, not a quick fix.
